Privacy Policy

1. Scope of Collection Information

- We collect personal information from you when:

You provide it straight to us. The personal information you provide to us is primarily done on the website of Swanglee Bookstore. This information includes your full name, email address, phone number, address, account information , including any information required to set up an account such as a username, login password, login ID/address, and security question/answer.

- Cookies:

When you interact with the swangleebookstore.com website, we collect information through cookies and other tracking technologies. We may get personal information from other legally permissible sources.

2. Storage duration

Customer's personal information will be kept on file until the customer requests cancellation or logs in and cancels themselves. Your personal information will be kept private on Swanglee Bookstore's servers at all times.

3. Data Security

We constantly aim to keep our clients' personal information secure, and we have implemented several security procedures, including:

Ensure operational safety: we store no personal information about our clients in a secure operating environment that is only accessible to workers, representatives, and service providers with a need-to-know basis. We secure our clients' personal information in accordance with industry norms and legislation.

If the information server is hacked by hackers, resulting in the loss of personal data from customers, we will be responsible for reporting the event to authorities for prompt investigation, managing, and alerting consumers.

Payment information is safeguarded in accordance with industry standards.
